🎯
A full-stack intelligent farming platform that combines real-time weather data, AI-based disease detection, government market data & rule-based advisory systems to help Indian farmers make data-driven crop decisions.
❓ Problem Statement

Indian farmers face critical challenges:

  • No data-driven guidance for crop selection
  • Late detection of plant diseases
  • Inefficient water & resource usage
  • No access to real-time market prices
  • Lack of early pest/disease warnings
  • Language barriers in tech tools
⭐ Key Features (18 Modules)
🌱
AI Crop Recommendation
Score-match 18 crops against soil NPK, pH & weather. Shows top 8 matches.
🔬
AI Disease Detection
Upload leaf photo → MobileNetV2 identifies 15 diseases across 3 crops.
💧
Smart Irrigation
28-day watering schedule based on crop, soil & live weather data.
📊
Market Prices & Forecast
Live mandi prices from 7,000+ mandis + 4-week price predictions.
🐛
Pest & Disease Alerts
34 weather-based rules to identify pest risk with severity ranking.
📈
Yield Prediction
Harvest estimates from soil, weather, irrigation & sowing season data.
🗺️
Interactive Farm Map
Leaflet.js map with clickable farm popups & GPS coordinates.
🧪
Soil Lab Locator
Find nearest soil testing labs via GPS with Haversine distance.
